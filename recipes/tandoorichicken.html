Italian Calzone Recipe

Tandoori chicken is a dish made from chicken marinated in yogurt and spices and roasted in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven. The dish is now popular worldwide. The modern form of the dish was popularized by the Moti Mahal restaurant in New Delhi, India in the late 1940s.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Tandoori spice blend
  • Lemon
  • Coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and tandoori spice.
  2. Marinate chicken in the mixture for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Place marinated chicken on a baking tray.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until cooked.
  6. Garnish with lemon and cilantro. Serve hot.
